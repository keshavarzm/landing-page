Features

Roundee.io steps up your meeting game. Everything you need in one
easy-to-use platform, instant and free.

Unlimited Calls

Have as many video conference calls as you want.

Recording

One-hour free recording per meeting for the host only.

Invitation by Email

Once the meeting starts, invite guest by email.

Host controls

Admin can manage guest mic settings.

Receive Recorded Meetings Instantly

After the call, access your recorded videos by email or by message in Slack.

Timestamped Notes

Easily create timestamped notes to mark down meeting highlights.

Browser-based

No installation or plug-in required to start or join a call.

No Sign-Up

Host and guests don’t need to sign up to start a video call.

Invitation by Link

Invite guests by sending a link (URL) to join the call.

Screen Sharing

The host or a guest can share their screen to make a presentation.

Cloud Storage

Each recorded meeting is saved in cloud storage for 2 weeks.

Multi Modal Supported

Start or join a meeting from desktop or mobile.

Slack Integration

Install roundee.io to your Slack channel in 3 simple steps.

Start a Call from Slack

Just type the /roundee slash command in either a Slack Channel or a Direct Message.

Chat During Calls

All conservations are saved in a personal web page.

Download and Share video

Download is available in mp4 format. Share a meeting video by copy and pasting the web page URL.
